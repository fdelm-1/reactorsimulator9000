TEMPERATURE CONTROL

temperature proportional to power output
-> although slow change

Temperature increase cases:
    move the rods too fast
    control rods removed

Temperature decrease cases:
    control rods inserted

High temperature consequences:
    Unstable power output (due to component damage)
        add jitter
    longer scrams
-> have some alert that instructs player to control switches

5 switches controlling coolant flow:
    Top one - largest effect
    Next two moderate, bottom two low effect


prevent players from leaving all 5 coolant switches on at all times
through 'overcooling shock'
    If all switches are turned on while power is low, power surges
