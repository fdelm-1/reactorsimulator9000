==============================================================================
REACTOR SIMULATOR 9000 - QUICK START
==============================================================================

This is a short, step-by-step guide to actually playing the game. For full
details (controls reference, hardware wiring, config tuning, code layout)
see README.txt instead - this file is just the play-through steps.


STEP 1 - START THE PUMPS
----------------------------
  1. Turn ON all five coolant-pump switches.
  2. Press and HOLD the LEFT button for 2 seconds.
  3. Check the "COOLANT PUMPS" panel above the graph - all five boxes
     should now be lit green. You can let go of the left button now.

  (If you don't see green boxes, a switch was off during the hold - turn
  them all on and hold the left button again.)


STEP 2 - BRING THE REACTOR CRITICAL
----------------------------------------
  1. Push all three levers (safety, regulating, chemical shim) all the way
     DOWN.
  2. Press the RIGHT button.
  3. The reactor should now start - the graph, vessel diagram and dial will
     come to life.


STEP 3 - RAISE POWER TOWARDS THE TARGET
--------------------------------------------
  The reactor starts fully shut down (all rods in), so power will sit near
  zero until you withdraw rods. Slowly pull the levers back UP (safety and
  regulating levers do most of the work; the chemical-shim lever is a
  smaller, faster trim) and watch the power line on the graph climb.

  Aim for the GREEN band on the graph (192-208 MW by default). Go gently -
  it's easy to overshoot.


STEP 4 - HOLD IT THERE
----------------------------
  Once the power line is inside the green band, watch the "Time at target"
  readout on the graph. It counts up while you're inside the band and resets
  to zero the moment you leave it - so you need 5 unbroken seconds inside
  the band to win, not 5 seconds total.

  Make small lever adjustments to stay inside the band rather than large
  ones - large moves are what cause you to overshoot back out of it.


STEP 5 - WATCH THE WARNINGS
--------------------------------
  While holding position, keep an eye on:
    - The power graph's amber/red bands - if power gets too high, the
      reactor SCRAMs automatically.
    - The "TEMPERATURE WARNING" banner above the dial - if it starts
      flashing, fuel temperature is getting close to its own automatic
      SCRAM limit. Turning on more pumps cools things down, but very
      slightly reduces k_eff (power-raising ability) too.

  If a SCRAM happens (automatic or by pressing the left button/SPACE
  yourself): the reactor is not dead, but the SCRAM rods slam in and you
  lose k_eff. To recover: push all three levers all the way back down and
  wait a few seconds - only once BOTH of those are true does control return
  to you and the SCRAM rods start withdrawing again. Then go back to step 3.


STEP 6 - WIN
----------------
  Hold the power line inside the green band for a full 5 continuous seconds
  and the reactor shuts down automatically. Type a name (up to 12
  characters) and press Enter to save your time to the leaderboard, or press
  Escape to clear what you've typed. Leaving the name blank when you press
  Enter records "Anonymous" and does NOT save a leaderboard entry.


STEP 7 - PLAY AGAIN
------------------------
  Press the RIGHT button (or 'R' on a keyboard) to restart and try to beat
  your time.


==============================================================================
QUICK REFERENCE
==============================================================================
  LEFT button (before start)  - hold 2s with all switches on: spin up pumps
  LEFT button (while running) - manual SCRAM
  RIGHT button (before start) - start the reactor (needs pumps on + all
                                 switches on + all levers fully down)
  RIGHT button (after a win, or while running) - restart

  Levers UP   = no effect on power (k_eff at its highest)
  Levers DOWN = reduce power (k_eff lower) - push down to control an
                overshoot, or after a SCRAM to bring the lock back up

  Keyboard equivalents (desktop / testing only):
    T          - start the game directly
    SPACE or 0 - manual SCRAM
    W/S        - raise/lower power (only when lever control is off)
    R          - restart
    Q          - quit
